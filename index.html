About Our Events

Open IIT

After a week long Induction program ,freshers are welcomed by the first event of technology students Gymkhana giving opportunity to all newly enrolled UG & PG students to be a part of Athletics team. A certified competition with medals to podium finishers.

Inter Hall

Beginning of Inter-Hall Championship is marked by this event. The most important sporting event for any athlete to be a part of Inter IIT athletics family. With competition like 100m ,200m, relay race, medley, shot-put, etc. witnesses great tempo & participation from all Halls of Residences.

Pentathlon & trithalon

With the beginning of the new year, hopping that the resolves of students are strong, with aim to achieve their fitness goals, Technology Students’ Gymkhana bring the perfect opportunity to realise their true potential with the Open IIT Pentathlon for boys and Triathlon for girls.
